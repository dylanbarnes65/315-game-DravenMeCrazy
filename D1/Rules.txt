Rules: 
Players each have their own lane.
Players must stay in their own lane
Can have multiple turrets in one lane, but only one per row
Enemies can only appear at the opposite side of the screen.
Bosses will appear every 10th wave   



Mechanics:
Players can only move up and down in there lane by using the W(up) and S(down) keys
Players can attack by pressing space
Enemies always walk straight ahead
Enemies can only attack up against the wall
Players can upgrade their weapons
Enemies will get stronger during the course of the game.
Players can only move on to next wave if all enemies are defeated. 

Victory Conditions:
For the player or players to win they must complete all waves.
Enemies win if they manage to bring down the wall to zero hp causing the player or players to lose the game
